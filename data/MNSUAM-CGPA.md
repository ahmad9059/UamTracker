## **Formula for Calculation GPA and CGPA**

- **GPA** =  
    _Sum of total quality points per semester ÷ Sum of total credit hours per semester_
    
- **CGPA** =  
    _Sum of total quality points from 1st to end semester ÷ Sum of total credit hours from 1st to end semester_
